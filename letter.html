Professional Profile and Personal Statement

Hello,

I am Costantine Mapiye, a Zimbabwean professional based in South Africa and currently serving as Business and Marketing Manager at New Balance Cape Town. Over the past six years, I have built my career across retail leadership, brand strategy, team development, and customer engagement within the sports and lifestyle sector.

This portfolio is intended to offer a balanced picture of who I am, both professionally and personally. It highlights the work I lead, the communities I engage with, and the lifestyle that shapes my perspective as a manager and brand builder.

  • I lead retail and marketing operations across New Balance's Cape Town flagship locations.
  • I focus on connecting strong commercial performance with authentic brand storytelling.
  • I value community, fitness, and culture as essential parts of modern lifestyle marketing.
  • I believe leadership is most effective when it combines strategy, consistency, and people development.

Professionally, I am motivated by building memorable customer experiences, developing high-performing teams, and translating global brand values into locally relevant campaigns. My role requires both analytical discipline and creativity, and I take pride in balancing the operational side of retail with the emotional side of brand identity.

Beyond work, I maintain an active lifestyle that reflects the values of the industry I represent. Running, community participation, travel, and time spent exploring Cape Town's culture all influence the way I approach leadership, marketing, and personal growth.

  • Cape Town is central to both my current career and daily life.
  • My work is closely tied to people, partnerships, and communities on the ground.
  • I enjoy environments where sport, culture, and business intersect naturally.
  • I aim to keep growing as a leader while staying grounded in authenticity and discipline.

The pages that follow provide more context on my professional engagements, travel interests, and lifestyle, offering a broader sense of the experiences and values that define my profile.

Thank you for taking the time to view the portfolio.

Kind regards,
