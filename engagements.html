Professional Engagements

A chronological record of key business activities and milestones.

🔴 Current Focus — 2026

Leading the Cape Town winter collection launch campaign across both flagship stores while deepening local brand partnerships, community initiatives, and high-performance retail storytelling.

Jan 2026 — Present
2026 Winter Campaign Launch — New Balance Cape Town
Spearheading the full strategy and execution of New Balance's winter 2026 collection launch across V&A Waterfront and Canal Walk stores. Campaign includes in-store activations, influencer partnerships, and digital ad spend optimisation across Meta and Google platforms. Early Q1 sales results show a 22% increase compared to the same period in 2025.
October 2025
NB South Africa Regional Manager's Conference — Johannesburg
Presented Cape Town retail performance data and strategic insights at the biannual NB South Africa Regional Managers' Conference held in Sandton, Johannesburg. Delivered a segment on consumer behaviour trends in the Western Cape market and co-facilitated a workshop on digital-first marketing approaches for physical retail stores.
August 2025
Cape Town Sanlam Cape Town Marathon — NB Brand Activation
Managed end-to-end brand activation for New Balance at the Sanlam Cape Town Marathon — one of the most prestigious road running events in Africa. The activation included a branded experiential zone, product showcase, athlete meet-and-greet, and live social media coverage. Over 3,200 brand touchpoints logged and 280+ pairs of Fresh Foam X sold on event day.
June 2025
New Balance x Local Athlete Partnership Programme
Initiated and concluded partnership agreements with three emerging Cape Town-based athletes (running, basketball, and street culture) to serve as local NB brand ambassadors. Developed ambassador briefs, content calendars, and performance KPIs. Campaign generated R480,000 in attributed digital-driven revenue within the first 60 days.
March 2025
Canal Walk Store Refresh & Concept Rollout
Oversaw the full concept refresh of the Canal Walk New Balance store including new fixture layouts, in-store storytelling panels, and a dedicated "Heritage Wall" celebrating NB's Boston origins. The project was completed on time and under budget, resulting in a 31% uplift in average basket value in the month post-launch.
November 2024
Black Friday Campaign — R2.1M Revenue Weekend
Planned and executed New Balance Cape Town's most successful Black Friday campaign to date, generating R2.1M in combined weekend sales across both locations. Strategy included countdown digital campaigns, exclusive pre-launch VIP access, and a staff training blitz the week prior to ensure peak in-store experience delivery.
2023 — 2024
NB Running Community — Cape Town Launch
Founded and now oversees the New Balance Running Community in Cape Town — a free weekly community run anchored at the V&A Waterfront store. The initiative has grown to over 340 registered members and serves as a customer retention, brand advocacy, and community-building platform. The programme is now used as a case study by NB South Africa national marketing team.
2022
Promoted to Business & Marketing Manager
Promoted from Senior Sales Consultant to Business and Marketing Manager, assuming full responsibility for both Cape Town flagship locations. Role encompasses strategic marketing planning, business performance reporting, partner and supplier management, team leadership (12 staff), and direct reporting to the NB South Africa National Retail Director.
2020 — 2022
Senior Sales Consultant — New Balance V&A Waterfront
Joined New Balance at the V&A Waterfront store as a Senior Sales Consultant. Consistently ranked in the top 3 performers nationally within NB South Africa's retail network. Led in-store training initiatives and was appointed as the store's social media coordinator in early 2021.
